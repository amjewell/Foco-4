How it Works

List!

First things first. Pick out the outfit you wore last weekend- one that's cute, trendy, and fun, but you’re not ready to re-wear just yet. Upload pictures of this to the app and start looking at what other people have listed.

Step 1

Exchange!

After the app matches you with someone to trade you will be assigned a local meet up spot to exchange! You will do the same thing at the end of the weekend to trade back!

Step 2

Wear!

Take on the world with the confidence that can only be found in a new outfit! Enjoy a weekend filled with new outfits and feel great about saving money and the environment at the same time!
